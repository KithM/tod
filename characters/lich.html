The Lich (BBEG)

Once a revered Magi (Magister Eldryn), celebrated for his unparalleled wisdom and formidable power, he met a tragic demise. In the aftermath of his downfall, the Undying Cult chose to reanimate him as a Lich, intending to unleash vengeance upon the lands. He now exists as an unwilling pawn, an escalating menace to the realm.
